BLACKSMITHING SMELTING SCRIPT (VORP CORE)
-----------------------------------------

- Walk near a forge (prop p_forge01x) and press E.
- Choose what ore you want to smelt.
- Progress bar + animation plays.
- Ore is removed, bar is added.

Setup:
- Drop into resources folder.
- Add to server.cfg.
- Make sure you have vorp_inventory, feather_menu, and feather_progressbar and vorp_animations.

Author: FatherHarold
Enjoy!
